Will plants grow under light?

The short answer is: Yes, plants can grow under artificial light. Light is one of the essential factors for plant growth, as it drives the process of photosynthesis. While sunlight is the natural source of light for most plants, artificial light sources like fluorescent lights, LED lights, and high-intensity discharge (HID) lamps can also provide the necessary light spectrum for plants to grow.
